Our Mission:

While we understand that not everyone will be able to go to their number one school, we make it our mission to give each and every one of our consultees the maximum chance of being accepted in the boarding school of their choice. With our staff members having gone to boarding school, we understand the unique memories that are created there and wish to share these fruitful experiences with as many students as we can. Additionally, we want to make the process less confusing as we have been through the strenuous process ourselves. The application is difficult to complete alone, so we offer services to aid anybody who needs help.

What is boarding school?

Boarding schools can take many forms (refer to the next question). A typical boarding school offers dormitories which allows students who live far away to spend their schoolyear on campus. Boarding schools take in an array of students who not only come from different states but also different countries. These schools hire the best teachers possible giving students an education more rigorous than public school. Class size is smaller compared to most other schools and total population is also significantly lower.
